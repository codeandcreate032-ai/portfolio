About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

Kendriya Vidyalaya, Vizag Steel Plant was established in 1983 under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan, Ministry of Education.

Located in Ukkunagaram, Visakhapatnam, within the Vizag Steel Plant Township, we've been providing quality education for nearly four decades.

Our commitment to holistic development nurtures young minds with academic excellence, cultural values, and physical fitness.
