About Me

I'm B. Mohith, a final year Diploma student in the AIM (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) branch, based in Visakhapatnam Steel Plant Area. I completed my schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya Steel Plant from 2013 to 2023, and I'm set to complete my Diploma in March/April 2026.

My journey has been a unique blend of sports and technology. As a national-level athlete in roller skating and inline hockey, I've learned discipline, perseverance, and teamwork - qualities that I now apply to my tech pursuits.

I'm a certified Full-Stack Developer with expertise in C, Python, JavaScript, MySQL, and various web technologies. Beyond coding, I'm passionate about AI/ML, Arduino robotics, and creative technologies. Through my "CodeandCreate" channel, I share my knowledge and projects with a growing community of tech enthusiasts.
